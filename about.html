About Us

Company Overview

CLOCKSY Pvt.Ltd is one of the leading electronics distributors in Sri Lanka that has grown from strength to strength since its establishment.


Incorporated in 2018, the company started with humble beginnings but with sound knowledge on the smartwatches and strong leadership skills, CLOCKSY today is recognised as a strong competitor in the smartwatche market, having started with offering quality smartwatches, and smartwatch accessories.


CLOCKSY Pvt.Ltd is a licenced importer and distributor of smartwatches and has a plethora of offerings in its portfolio.


While in its product range are top brands, CLOCKSY keeps abreast with new technological developments and strives to introduce the same to the local market at its earliest.


With the objective of making technology accessible to as many as possible, CLOCKSY makes available its products at affordable rates, ensuring a greater proportion of the population can reap the benefits of the latest technology available.


A key priority for CLOCKSY is the affordability factor, and the same is achieved without compromising quality. All imported products are of the highest standard.


To ensure its business is in line with the current times, CLOCKSY continues to invest time and resources to conceptualise ways in which it can provide the very best, latest technology, for the right price.
